About K&Y Jewellery

Our Story

Welcome to K&Y Jewellery, where elegance meets craftsmanship. Founded in 2010, we have over a decade of experience in creating exquisite jewelry that captures the essence of beauty and timelessness. Our journey began with a passion for designing unique pieces that tell a story, celebrate love, and mark life’s most precious moments.

At K&Y Jewellery, we believe that every piece of jewelry should be as unique as the person wearing it. That’s why we work closely with skilled artisans to craft collections that blend modern trends with classic designs, ensuring that each piece is a work of art.

Our Team

Our team is a family of passionate designers, craftsmen, and jewelry enthusiasts dedicated to bringing you the best. Led by our founders, Karen and Yvonne, we combine creativity and expertise to create jewelry that resonates with our customers. From sourcing the finest materials to perfecting every detail, our team is committed to excellence.

Karen - Co-Founder & Designer

Yvonne - Co-Founder & Creative Director

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the finest materials to ensure every piece is of the highest standard.
  • Creativity: Our designs are inspired by the beauty of nature, love, and modern elegance.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your happiness is our priority, and we strive to provide exceptional service.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices in all our operations.
